EDSA Official Website

Digital Transformation for UIN Ar-Raniry's English Department Student Association.

The Challenge

EDSA UIN Ar-Raniry needed a central digital hub to manage information for over 1,000 students. The previous method of relying solely on social media resulted in fragmented information and difficulty in archiving organizational achievements.

The Solution

We engineered a responsive web platform focusing on accessibility and professional branding. The new site serves as a one-stop portal for news, events, and organizational structure transparency.

Fast Performance

Optimized asset loading ensuring smooth navigation even on slower networks.

Smart Gallery

Masonry layout algorithm that beautifully arranges varied image sizes.
