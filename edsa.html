Project Context & Impact

The Challenge: EDSA UIN Ar-Raniry needed a central digital hub to manage information for over 1,000 students. The previous method of relying solely on social media resulted in fragmented information and difficulty in archiving organizational achievements.

The Solution: We engineered a responsive web platform focusing on accessibility and professional branding. The new site serves as a one-stop portal for news, events, and organizational structure transparency.
